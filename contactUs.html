    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)

    Cancel my booking

    Log in → Bookings → “Manage”. You can cancel or reschedule up to 24 h before pickup.

    Security deposit

    A refundable ₹3 000 hold is placed on your card; it’s released 48 h after the car is returned.

    Required documents

    Bring a valid driving licence, the card used for payment, and any government ID for verification.

    Fuel policy

    Return the vehicle with the same fuel level it had at pickup to avoid refill charges.
