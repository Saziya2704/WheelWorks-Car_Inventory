    Cancellation & Refund policy

    What if my plans change?
    Booking Cancellation: Cancellations made 24 hours before pickup are fully refundable. Cancellations within 24 hours incur a 50% charge. No refund for last-minute cancellations or no-shows.

    Refund Processing: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. The amount will be credited to the original payment method. Processing time may vary based on the bank.

    Modification Requests: Booking modifications are allowed based on availability. Date or vehicle changes may incur additional charges. Contact support for modifications before the rental period.
